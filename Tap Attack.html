Tap Attack

Tap Attack is a game that I have made in a week long gamejam using Unreal Engine 5. The theme for this gamejam was "bosses", so I created a boss tapping game, you simply click and you attack, every time you kill the boss it gets stronger, and every time u gain a level you would gain stronger, the game is pretty difficult, but games aren't fun with a bit of challenge, I my self have not been able to get past level 7. He gets really strong at that time. (Even tho I had to nerf the boss a bit), anyways, make sure not to die yourself, as you do lose quite a bit of EXP and Gold (No use yet), and if I remember correctly, the boss also gets stronger every time you die, so have fun with this little game I created!
