GameJamTPS3

This is a 1 week long game jam that I made for the character "Cavalryman", cavalry man is a zombie that kills people. In my version of him, he is hunted by police and people with bats, who are all trying to kill him, however you are much stronger than them and can run faster than them, your goal is to survive as long as possible, the longer you survive, the more difficult it becomes. Every hit you do gives you exp, every level you gain makes you faster and stronger and last longer. Have some fun and see how long you'll last with this little game I've made!
