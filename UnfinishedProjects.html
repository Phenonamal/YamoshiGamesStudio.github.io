Anime Legends

Anime Legends is a Turn Based Anime Gacha Game with cubed like models. This game I've never gotten to fully complete, however it was a fun idea , but the scope was too huge for a solo person to complete. Perhaps if I've gotten people to help me with the project I could've completed it, or gotten much further than now. Right now the game has the main principles of the concepts done, you can summon characters and fight, but there's no skills for the characters and there's only 6 characters in the game. The saving/Loading data isn't fully finished yet either, hopefully when I get the time, I'll look at it again and improve it, but as of now I don't.
